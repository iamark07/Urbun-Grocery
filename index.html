Franchise Models We Offer

Choose the model that fits your vision and investment level — from compact kiosks to full-scale stores.

Express Kiosk

Compact retail unit ideal for high-footfall areas like metro stations or malls.

  • 100–200 sq.ft.
  • Investment: ₹3–5 Lakhs
  • Setup Time: 15 Days

Mini Supermarket

Ideal for residential areas and local shopping complexes.

  • 300–600 sq.ft.
  • Investment: ₹8–12 Lakhs
  • Setup Time: 25 Days

Hyper Store

Full-fledged store for premium areas and high-volume sales.

  • 1000+ sq.ft.
  • Investment: ₹18–25 Lakhs
  • Setup Time: 30–40 Days
